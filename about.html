About Me

Currently, I work as a Production/Graphic Designer for Pentera, Inc. where I develop marketing material for capital campaigns to raise awareness for nonprofit organizations. I specialize in UX/UI development, utilizing simplicity and functionality to optimize user experiences.

People inspire my work because the way we think shapes the user experience. Conversations, podcasts, and MBTI psychology enrich my understanding of people.

I spend my free time exploring culture and architecture through travel, books, and vlogs. Every city I visit expands my understanding of people, fueling my passion for creating comprehensive user experiences.
